About Bohol

Bohol is a first-class municipality in the province of Bohol, Philippines. It is located in the central part of the province, known for its natural beauty and historical significance. The municipality serves as a major tourist destination and a commercial center in the region.

Bohol is named after the province itself, rich with heritage and natural wonders. The municipality's strategic location has made it a hub for tourism, trade, and commerce in the region.

Bohol is known for its vibrant agricultural economy, with rice, corn, coconut, and various tropical fruits as major crops. The municipality is also establishing itself as an educational and cultural center with several institutions and historical landmarks.

With a diverse population, Bohol is a community that celebrates its rich cultural heritage through various festivals and events throughout the year, including vibrant celebrations that showcase the municipality's arts, culture, and agricultural products.

The municipality is led by the local government unit headed by the Municipal Mayor, along with the Vice Mayor and members of the Sangguniang Bayan (Municipal Council).

Municipal Services

Business Permits and Licensing

The Municipality of Bohol provides efficient processing of business permits, licenses, and other related documents. Our Business Permits and Licensing Office (BPLO) is committed to providing quality service to all business owners in the municipality.

Business Hours: Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM

Civil Registry

The Municipal Civil Registry Office manages and records vital statistics including births, deaths, marriages, and other civil registry documents. We ensure that all records are well-maintained and accessible to citizens when needed.

Available Services: Birth Certificate, Marriage Certificate, Death Certificate, CENOMAR, and other civil registry documents.

Health Services

The Municipal Health Office provides comprehensive health services to residents, including medical consultations, maternal and child health care, immunization, and nutrition programs. Our team of healthcare professionals is dedicated to ensuring the well-being of the entire community.

Regular Health Programs: Immunization, Maternal Care, Dental Services, Nutrition Programs, and more.

Agriculture Support

The Municipal Agriculture Office provides technical assistance, resources, and support to farmers and agricultural workers. We offer various programs aimed at improving agricultural productivity, sustainability, and income generation.

Programs: Farm Input Subsidies, Technical Training, Market Linkages, and Crop Insurance Assistance.

Social Welfare Services

The Municipal Social Welfare and Development Office provides assistance to vulnerable sectors of the community including children, elderly, persons with disabilities, and families in crisis situations. We implement various social protection programs and services.

Services: Financial Assistance, Livelihood Programs, Community Development, and Crisis Intervention.

Disaster Risk Reduction

The Municipal Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Office (MDRRMO) is responsible for implementing disaster preparedness, prevention, mitigation, response, and recovery programs. We work to ensure the safety and resilience of the community during emergencies.

Activities: Disaster Drills, Emergency Response Training, Early Warning Systems, and Relief Operations.
